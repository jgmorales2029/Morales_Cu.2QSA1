El Nido

El Nido town is renowned for its breathtaking rock formations, secluded lagoons, and pristine beaches. As one of the premier island destinations globally, El Nido captivates visitors with its striking limestone cliffs, white sand beaches, and vibrant turquoise waters.

Coron

Coron is a large island that is part of the Palawan archipelago in the Philippines. It is a cute island from where you can take nice boat trips to beautiful lakes and other islands. In contrast to the rest of Palawan, Coron is reminiscent of Italy in terms of landscape. It is slightly less tropical.

Puerto Princesa Underground River

The park features a striking limestone karst landscape with an underground river flowing into the sea, affected by tides. It plays a vital role in biodiversity conservation, showcasing a comprehensive mountain-to-ocean ecosystem.
