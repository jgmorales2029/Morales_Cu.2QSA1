Kinilaw

Kinilaw is a Filipino ceviche, made of raw fish or seafood marinated in vinegar, citrus juice, and seasonings. In Palawan, it often features local fish like tanigue or tuna, along with coconut milk, ginger, onions, and chili.

Nido Soup

Palawan is renowned for its edible bird's nest, essential for the traditional Nido Soup. Valued for its delicate texture and nutritional benefits, Nido Soup is often enjoyed on special occasions, reflecting the region's culinary richness.
